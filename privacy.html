Glotty Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 June 2026

Glotty (歌蒂) is a menu-bar translation and language-learning app for macOS. This policy explains exactly what data Glotty handles. The short version: Glotty has no account, shows no ads, and runs no analytics — and your text never passes through our servers, because we don't operate any.

What Glotty does with the text you select

  • Translate runs on your Mac using Apple's built-in translation. The selected text is not sent to us or to any third party.
  • Explain, Polish, and Chat send the relevant text to the AI provider you choose and configure yourself (for example OpenAI, DeepSeek, or Grok), using the API key you entered. The request goes directly from your Mac to that provider. Glotty has no server in between and keeps no copy. How that provider handles the text is governed by their privacy policy.

What is stored, and where

  • Your API keys are stored locally on your Mac in the macOS Keychain. They are sent only to the provider they belong to.
  • Learned vocabulary, history, and settings are stored locally on your Mac. They are not uploaded anywhere.
  • Glotty does not collect your name, email, contacts, location, device identifiers, usage analytics, or crash diagnostics.

Third-party AI providers

When you enable Explain, Polish, or Chat, the text you act on is sent to the provider you configured. Glotty does not control those services. Please review the privacy policy of whichever provider you connect (for example OpenAI, DeepSeek, or xAI/Grok) to understand how they process your data.

Permissions Glotty requests

  • Accessibility — to read the text you have selected in the active app, so it can be translated or processed.
  • Input Monitoring — to detect the Fn-leader keyboard shortcut. Glotty does not record or store your keystrokes.

Children

Glotty is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date above.
